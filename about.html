About

Karina

Wedding Photographer

I have spent the last decade quietly following couples through the most important day of their lives — not to direct it, but to witness it. My approach is documentary at heart: I stay close, stay unobtrusive, and let the day unfold. What I bring home are images that feel less like photographs and more like memory — the kind you reach for when words fall short.

I work in natural light whenever possible and believe that the truest moments happen in the margins: the five minutes before the ceremony, the last slow dance when the room has mostly emptied, the way two people look at each other when they think no one is watching. Those are the frames I chase. Based in New York, I travel worldwide and welcome enquiries from anywhere.
